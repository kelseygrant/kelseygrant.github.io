My Experiences



Product Implementation Intern

FIS

As a rising Junior in college, I was just beginning my journey into the world of technology and how it applies to the corporate environment. At FIS, I dove headfirst into innovative projects, such as working hands-on with their unique lending system-- an advanced software system used in banks for more efficient lending services. Within my team, we kept clients' information up to date, as well as built layouts for lending documents, and inputting user-content into the system for our FLO product in order aid clients and administrators with navigation. After my internship ended, as I departed back to school, I recieved an offer to stay with the company for the following school-year, working long-distance as their implementations intern-- when I continued those same tasks along with managing the conversion project as the company transitioned into a new system.



IT Business Relationship-Football Solutions Intern

NFL

As I boarded the plane to New York City, on my way to intern for the National Football League, reality was starting to sink in. What seemed like a dream before was becoming reality. It was not only eye-opening, but an amazing learning experience, to work among some of the best talent within the sports industry-- developing user manuals, project-managing, and user-testing for the very company that I had idolized for years. I had the opportunity to travel to Dallas, Texas to assist the Officiating Clinic with managing the Surface 3 inventory process. Along with this, I stood alongside the NFL headquarter staff on game day to support them as technology and sports came to a collision during the Preseason Game 1, where we made oversaw and managed every piece of technology used in that game. This was, by far, one of the most rewarding experiences I have had, and I hope for many others to come in the future.
